Are you excited about the upcoming launch of Saros? This guide breaks down the release times for both the Deluxe and Standard Editions, ensuring you know exactly when to dive into the action. For those who pre-ordered the Deluxe Edition, you can start playing on April 27 at 9:00 PM Pacific Time, with subsequent times rolling out across major cities worldwide. Standard Edition players will have to wait until April 29 to join the fun. Additionally, pre-loading is available for those who have pre-ordered the digital version, allowing you to jump right into the game as soon as it launches.
