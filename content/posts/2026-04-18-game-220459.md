The 2026 BAFTA Games Awards showcased remarkable talent in the gaming industry, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 taking home the coveted Best Game award. This year's ceremony highlighted a diverse range of titles, emphasizing the creativity and innovation that continues to shape the gaming landscape.

Clair Obscur not only won Best Game but also secured victories in Debut Game and Performer in a Leading Role for Jennifer English, marking a significant achievement for the title. Meanwhile, Dispatch, the superhero narrative from AdHoc, also made waves by winning three awards, including Animation and Audio Achievement, further proving the depth of storytelling and artistry in modern games.
