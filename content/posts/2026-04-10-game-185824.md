In a shocking revelation, Gunzilla Games, the blockchain game studio co-founded by filmmaker Neill Blomkamp, is facing serious allegations from former employees regarding unpaid wages. Reports indicate that some staff members have not received their salaries for several months, raising concerns about the company's financial practices and treatment of its workforce.

Former animator Paul Creamer took to LinkedIn to share his experience, claiming he hasn't been paid since October 2025. He described a culture of silence within the company, where management allegedly promised timely payments but failed to deliver. This situation has resonated with other employees, who echoed similar grievances, suggesting a systemic issue within the organization.

As the gaming industry increasingly embraces blockchain technology and NFTs, the treatment of artists and developers remains a critical topic. Will Gunzilla Games address these allegations and restore trust among its employees, or will this be a cautionary tale for others in the blockchain gaming space?
