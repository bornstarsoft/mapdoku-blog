Get ready to embark on a whimsical adventure with the upcoming mobile puzzle game, Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster, set to launch on iOS and Android later this year. Developed by HAOPLAY, this casual game invites players to step into the webbed feet of Captain Quack, who must navigate a perilous ocean filled with monsters while managing a squad of unique birds. The innovative gameplay combines classic pachinko mechanics with strategic bird selection, promising a fresh experience for puzzle enthusiasts.

Players will have the opportunity to choose from a diverse roster of up to fifty birds, each belonging to one of six distinct classes. This variety not only enhances gameplay but also encourages experimentation with different strategies to tackle each level's challenges. With additional modes like the Purgatory Tower and Starry Expedition, players can hone their skills or compete against others, ensuring that there's always something new to explore.

As pre-registration opens, players can look forward to an array of exciting rewards, including exclusive in-game items and characters. With its engaging mechanics and vibrant world, Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster is poised to capture the hearts of gamers everywhere. Will you join Captain Quack on this epic journey?
