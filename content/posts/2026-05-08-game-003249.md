The Daily Mail has just launched Relink, a captivating new puzzle game designed to stimulate your lateral thinking skills. This daily brainteaser presents players with a grid of 16 words or phrases, where the challenge is to identify the four imposters that dont belong. But the twist? These imposters are cleverly linked by a common theme, making the game not just about finding mismatches but also about uncovering hidden connections.

Each day, players have four lives to solve the puzzle, with a new challenge released at midnight. The game encourages you to think outside the box and engage in wordplay, as the clues are often more nuanced than they appear. Tips from the Puzzles Editor suggest starting from the top row and vocalizing the words to reveal connections that might be overlooked.

As you dive into Relink, you'll find yourself not just playing a game but also exercising your brain in a fun and engaging way. Will you be able to keep your streak alive and compare your results with friends? This innovative approach to puzzles could redefine how we think about daily brainteasers.
