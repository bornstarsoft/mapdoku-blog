Get ready for an exciting new gaming experience as "Good Luck," a physics-based urban survival game, is set to debut on PC on April 8. This unique platformer challenges players to navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unusual hazards, all while trying to reach a meeting point. With both single-player and online co-op modes available for up to five players, it promises to deliver a thrilling adventure for friends and solo gamers alike.

Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games and published by Ultimate Games S.A., "Good Luck" is not just a game; it's an invitation to embrace chaos and creativity in gameplay. The game will also be available on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S, expanding its reach to console gamers. As we anticipate its release, one can't help but wonder how this innovative title will reshape the landscape of urban survival games. Will you be ready to tackle the challenges it presents?
