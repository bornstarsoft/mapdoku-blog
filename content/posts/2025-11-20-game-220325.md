In a captivating new commercial for the PGA Tour 2K on Nintendo Switch, Tiger Woods showcases his gaming skills alongside his caddie, sparking excitement among fans. The ad features Woods discussing the challenges of a par-3 hole while humorously interacting with his caddie, who quips, "Thats not what I have in mind," as they transition to gameplay footage. This clever blend of reality and gaming highlights Woods' enduring influence in the golf world, even as he faces challenges on the actual greens.

The trailer not only promotes the upcoming PGA Tour 2K25 but also serves as a reminder of Woods' legacy in golf, especially during a season where he has been sidelined due to injuries. While fans eagerly await his return to professional play, this commercial offers a glimpse of Woods in a different light, engaging with the gaming community and bringing a smile to his supporters.
