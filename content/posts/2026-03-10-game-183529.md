If you're a fan of cozy games and have a soft spot for cats, then "Kitty Haven" is a title you won't want to miss. This upcoming puzzle game invites players to create the perfect sanctuary for a variety of adorable and sometimes picky kittens. From solving unique puzzles to unlocking new items and residents, the game promises a delightful experience that combines strategy with creativity.

The demo is currently available on Steam, allowing players to dive into the charming world of Kitty Haven and experience the joy of arranging spaces for your furry friends. Whether your kittens are greedy, have sensitive noses, or are prone to cat fights, you'll need to think critically to restore peace and happiness in their little haven. As you progress, the game encourages you to express your creativity while ensuring the comfort of your feline companions.

As we look forward to its full release, Kitty Haven stands out as a testament to the growing trend of cozy gaming experiences that prioritize relaxation and creativity. Will you be one of the first to explore this enchanting world?
