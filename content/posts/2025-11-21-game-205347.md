The 43rd annual Golden Joystick Awards showcased the best in gaming, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 dominating the night by winning all seven awards it was nominated for, including Ultimate Game of the Year. This year's ceremony, held in London, saw over 21 million votes cast across 22 categories, highlighting the power of fan engagement in the gaming industry.

With standout titles like Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Yotei also receiving multiple nominations, the event not only celebrated individual achievements but also emphasized the growing diversity in gaming narratives and experiences. The awards serve as a reminder of how the gaming community can come together to recognize creativity and innovation.
