The highly anticipated sequel to the 2020 puzzle adventure game, Call of the Sea, titled Call of the Elder Gods, is set to launch on May 12, 2026. This new installment takes players on a thrilling journey 30 years after the original, introducing two new protagonists who must solve a cosmic mystery intertwined with their supernatural visions. With a narrative narrated by Norah Everhart, players will experience a rich story that blends exploration and puzzle-solving, all while drawing inspiration from the works of H.P. Lovecraft.

In Call of the Elder Gods, players will control Evangiline Drayton, a university student and engineer, alongside Harry Everhart, Norah's husband and an archaeology professor. The game maintains its first-person adventure style, allowing players to explore diverse locations, from the Australian Desert to the Arctic, while solving intricate puzzles. The ability to switch between characters adds a layer of strategy and depth to the gameplay, making it a must-play for fans of the genre.

As we await the game's release, excitement builds around how it will expand on the Lovecraftian themes established in the first game. Will Call of the Elder Gods elevate the narrative and gameplay experience even further? Only time will tell as we dive into this new cosmic adventure.
