The gaming community is buzzing with excitement as the main PlayStation Plus Essential game for April has been leaked ahead of Sony's official announcement. According to the reliable insider billbil-kun from Dealabs, the highly anticipated title is none other than *Lords of the Fallen*, the 2023 reboot of the classic dark fantasy action-RPG.

Originally released in October 2023, *Lords of the Fallen* has already made waves with its engaging gameplay and impressive sales figures, hitting one million copies sold just ten days post-launch. Players will step into the shoes of a Dark Crusader on a quest to defeat the demon God Adyr, promising a captivating experience for both new players and fans of the original.

This title will be available for PlayStation Plus subscribers from April 7 to May 5, alongside other exciting offerings. As always, once claimed, players can enjoy the game as long as they maintain their subscription.
