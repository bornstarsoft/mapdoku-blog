The gaming community is buzzing with the recent launch of Phase 2 for "Neverness to Everness," which introduces exciting new content despite the ongoing AI controversy that has surrounded the game since its global release. Hotta Studio has not only pushed forward with this update but has also enhanced the player experience with a new limited S-class character, Hotori, and a fresh arc banner, all available until June 3.

The gacha mechanics in "Neverness to Everness" have been designed with player-friendly features, such as the ability to carry over pity from one limited banner to the next. This approach is a refreshing change in the gacha landscape, where players often face resets and frustrations. With the introduction of the Bright Moon Special Arc Research Program and the Hourglass Perks event, players have even more reasons to engage with the game during this limited-time window.

As we look ahead, the anticipation for upcoming characters like Lacrimosa and Chaos adds to the excitement. Will these new additions help the game recover from the recent controversies? Only time will tell, but one thing is certain: the community remains eager for more content and innovation from Hotta Studio.
