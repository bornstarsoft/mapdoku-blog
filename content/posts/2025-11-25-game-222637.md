In a recent interview, former Rockstar writer Dan Houser shared his thoughts on the current state of AI in game development, emphasizing that while AI is making strides, it is not the panacea many claim it to be. Houser, who is working on his next game at Absurd Ventures, noted that although he is experimenting with AI, it still falls short of solving all the challenges faced in game design.

Houser pointed out that while AI can enhance certain aspects of gaming, it is not yet capable of addressing the myriad complexities involved in creating immersive experiences. He cautioned against the hype surrounding AI, suggesting that much of it is driven by commercial interests rather than genuine technological breakthroughs. The last 20% of achieving a perfect simulation remains a significant hurdle, and the industry must temper its expectations.
