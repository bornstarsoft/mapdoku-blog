As we look ahead to 2026, SEGA is gearing up for a double celebration, marking the 35th anniversaries of both Sonic the Hedgehog and Puyo Puyo. While Sonic has already made headlines, the announcement of the "Dairensa!" project for Puyo Puyo promises to bring a wave of nostalgia and excitement to fans of the beloved puzzle series. This initiative not only includes themed art and a special live stream event but also a series of collaborative activities that highlight the enduring popularity of these iconic games.

The Puyo Puyo 35th Anniversary Special live stream on February 1, 2026, is set to feature voice actors and live demos, providing fans with a unique glimpse into the franchise's future. Additionally, the pop-up stores across Japan will offer exclusive merchandise, making it a must-visit for enthusiasts. With the planned collaboration between Sonic and Puyo Puyo, its clear that SEGA is committed to celebrating its rich gaming history in a way that engages both longtime fans and new players alike.

As we await more announcements, one cant help but wonder: what surprises does SEGA have in store for us as these two beloved franchises celebrate their milestones together?
