Mojang's upcoming mobile title, Minecraft Blast, is generating buzz as it combines the beloved mechanics of Minecraft with the addictive gameplay of Candy Crush. This free-to-play puzzle game invites players to match blocks by color or type, offering quick and casual gaming sessions that are perfect for on-the-go entertainment. With a variety of boosts and themed areas to unlock, Minecraft Blast promises to deliver a unique experience that appeals to both Minecraft enthusiasts and casual gamers alike.

Currently in early testing phases, the game is being developed in collaboration with King Studios, the creators of Candy Crush Saga. Limited playtests have begun in select regions, with the first available on the Apple Store in Malaysia. As the team gathers user feedback, they aim to refine the game before its official release, ensuring that it meets the expectations of its diverse player base. Importantly, the development of Minecraft Blast will not interfere with ongoing updates and features for the original Minecraft game.
