Phantom Feline, the charming puzzle game developed by Shady Paw and published by the UMBC Game Developers Club, has just received a massive 2.0 update that significantly expands its gameplay. Drawing inspiration from classic Game Boy titles, this free 2D adventure allows players to step into the shoes of Shady the Ghost Cat, who navigates through puzzles using his unique shadow abilities. With the latest update, the games size has quadrupled, offering players 24 new levels and a plethora of new featuresall at no cost!

The 2.0 update introduces exciting new elements, including Steam Deck compatibility, multi-screen support, and a variety of new gameplay mechanics like conveyor belts and pressure pads. Players can now also enjoy enhanced graphics with new particle effects and a revamped user interface. With 14 new Steam achievements and the addition of post-game challenge modes, theres never been a better time to dive into the whimsical world of Phantom Feline.
