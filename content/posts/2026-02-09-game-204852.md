The upcoming release of "HER TREES: PUZZLE DREAM" promises to captivate puzzle enthusiasts with its unique point-and-click mechanics. Set to launch on February 10, 2026, this game invites players to engage with a dreamlike world where they can rearrange various objectslike scraps of paper and leavesto solve intricate puzzles. The innovative drag-and-drop system allows for free placement of items, encouraging creativity and exploration in a relaxing atmosphere.

As the third installment in the HER TREES series, this game builds on the success of its predecessor, "HER TREES: THE PUZZLE HOUSE," which garnered a Very Positive rating on Steam. With over 40 puzzles to tackle, players can expect a significant increase in content while enjoying a standalone experience that doesn't require prior knowledge of the series. The games non-verbal approach and monochromatic design ensure accessibility for all players, regardless of color vision.
