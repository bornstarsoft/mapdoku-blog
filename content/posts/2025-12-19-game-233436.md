Gamirror Games has unveiled an exciting new title, AiliA, a visual mirror-based puzzle game that promises to captivate players with its unique gameplay mechanics and stunning aesthetics. Set to launch on January 9, 2026, at 15:00 (UTC+8), AiliA invites players to step into a world where reality and reflection intertwine, offering a fresh take on the puzzle-adventure genre.

In AiliA, players will take on the role of Aria, a young girl on a quest to rescue her twin sister trapped in a mirrored realm. The gameplay revolves around manipulating mirrors to reshape the environment, creating pathways and unlocking new challenges. With over 100 meticulously crafted puzzles and seven visually distinct stages inspired by Eastern aesthetics, AiliA is designed to engage players' spatial awareness and creative thinking.

For those eager to dive into this enchanting world, a playable demo is currently available on Steam. As we approach the launch date, the anticipation for AiliA continues to build. Will this innovative puzzle game redefine the genre and capture the hearts of gamers worldwide? Only time will tell.
