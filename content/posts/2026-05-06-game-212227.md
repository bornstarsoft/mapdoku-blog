In a recent conversation, Alix Wilton Regan, the new voice of Lara Croft in the upcoming Tomb Raider games, passionately argued that video game actors deserve recognition for their contributions to the industry. She emphasized that the work of these performers is not only challenging but also vital in enhancing the overall gaming experience. As the industry evolves, it's crucial to acknowledge the artistry and dedication that goes into video game performances.

Regan highlighted the impact of the cast of Baldurs Gate 3, noting how their performances have inspired others in the industry to recognize their worth. This acknowledgment is long overdue, as many talented actors have historically been overlooked, often relegated to the background while their work is celebrated only in part. The call for more awards and recognition for motion capture artists is a step in the right direction, ensuring that all contributors to the gaming narrative are celebrated.

As we look to the future of gaming, its essential to continue advocating for the recognition of video game actors. Will we see more awards categories dedicated to performance capture, and how will this change the landscape of the gaming industry? The conversation is just beginning, and its one that we should all be a part of.
